Technical High School Management System

:Overview

The Technical High School Management System is a web-based application built to help high school students track their academic progress and receive the right support where needed.

Students can log in and indicate which subjects they are struggling with and which they are performing well in. Teachers can view student performance per subject and assign tutors or peer helpers to students who require additional assistance. Students are then able to see their assigned tutor or peer helper for each subject.

For Grade 10 students, the system also allows selection of a technical major (Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or Electrical Engineering) as well as a Math and Science track (Technical Math & Science or Pure Math & Science).

The goal of the system is to encourage collaboration, personalised academic support, and early identification of learning challenges.
